Proof, limits, and responsible use

What the evidence can and cannot say

Strong

Number symbolism is historically widespread.

Public references document number symbolism in philosophy, religion, art, alphabets, and cultural lucky/unlucky numbers.

Moderate

Numbers can structure reflection.

As a journaling framework, numerology can help people name themes, goals, tensions, and timing intentions.

Limited

Predictive claims are not scientifically established.

Psychology research on personal validation warns that broad personality statements may feel uniquely accurate even when they are generic.
