Privacy Policy

What We Collect

When you create an account, Firebase Authentication stores your email address, password credentials, user id, and optional display name. The numerology calculator runs in your browser and does not need sensitive identity information beyond what you choose to enter.

How We Use Data

  • To let you create, sign in to, and sign out of an account.
  • To provide numerology calculations and symbolic chart content.
  • To keep the site secure, debug issues, and improve the experience.

Advertising and Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, personalize, and measure ads. You can manage ad personalization through your Google account and browser privacy settings.

Firebase Services

The site is hosted on Firebase Hosting and uses Firebase Authentication. These services are provided by Google and may process technical data such as IP address, device information, and authentication activity as part of operating the service.

Ask B8 Chat

When you use the Ask chat, your question and relevant chart or profile context may be sent through Firebase AI Logic to a Google AI service to generate a response. Do not enter secrets or sensitive personal information into the chat.

Your Choices

  • You can use the calculator without creating an account.
  • You can sign out from the account menu.
  • You may request deletion of account-related data by contacting the site owner.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the B8 Numerology site owner through the account or project channel where you received access to this site.
